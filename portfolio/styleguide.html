I am a university student majoring in interactive art and technology with a concentration in design at Simon Fraser University. My passion lies in graphic design, web design, and UI design. With a proficiency in various software, including Python, Figma, Procreate, Protopie, HTML+CSS, Onshape, Rhino, Twinmotion, Java, and Processing. My basic design process involves extensive research, brainstorming, sketching, wireframing, prototyping. I believe that the beauty of design lies in not only meeting the needs of the user in terms of functionality, but also in making the product aesthetically pleasing. This combination of functionality and aesthetics is what I always strive for.
